Texas Hold'em Rules

Texas Hold'em is played with a standard 52-card deck. Each player is dealt two private cards, followed by five community cards placed face-up on the table.

Game Flow:

  1. Pre-flop: Each player receives two hole cards. Betting round begins.
  2. Flop: Three community cards are dealt. Betting round.
  3. Turn: Fourth community card is dealt. Betting round.
  4. River: Fifth community card is dealt. Final betting round.
  5. Showdown: Players reveal their hands, best hand wins.
